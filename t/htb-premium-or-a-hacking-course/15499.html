Hi There,

Personally i advise doing a course if you find yourself with limited time or in a situation where you are easy distracted with other responsibilities.

To answer your questions:

  1. What’s the quality of the HTB retired machine writeups? How in depth are they. Do they explain what the tools do? could they be used to learn more about the field and continue me on my way to becoming an absolute pen testing wizard?

The HTB walkthroughs are good quality, but that is all they are, walkthroughs. They sometime describe the when and the why but mostly they are a copy this and copy that and ta daa, you have the flag. Ippsec videos are amazing as you get a lot more context about why he does a thing and goes through the output. You can find a lot of these videos online but obviously need to get VIP to put it into practice.

  1. What are some good quality courses you would reccomend I put my money into? I’m not a noob, but I’m no where near being a pro, so whatever courses you can vouch for, begginner, advanced, or expert, Feel free to post them!

I would put them in this order when i am asked these questions:

For people starting out, Learn about the infrastructure you are trying to hack before pwning

Security +
CASP ( > CEH in my opinion (I don’t rate it at all))

If you are more than an intermediate then these…

QSTM or Check team member courses
OSCP
OSCE
