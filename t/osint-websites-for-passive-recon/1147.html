OSINT websites for passive recon

Reconnaissance
n0SiS September 28, 2016, 12:46am 1

Hi all, recently I have come across a couple of Open-Source Intel websites that I have come to find quite useful. I do not know how old or common they are but I have just recently found them;

https://www.punkspider.org - is a web application vulnerability search engine that scans for seven of the biggest web app vulnerabilities and puts the results into a search-able archive.

https://www.censys.io - is basically the same as shodan but a little more technical.

And in case there are any new newbies who do not already know, www.shodan.io is a great website to find new targets and perform passive recon.
